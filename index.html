Your stay, your rules

A tool that automatically plans your vacation based on your interests

User Interests

TripMan uses your chosen interests to make you a calender of events and happenings that you are guarunteed to enjoy.

Time Management

TripMan manages your time by strategically scheduling your events so that you have enough time inbetween to eat, sleep, and travel.

Customization

TripMan gives you the power to edit, add, or remove events to your liking, even after the algorithm has done its job.
